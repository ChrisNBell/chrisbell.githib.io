Loafing Sofa Cats

There's nothing cuter than cats who loaf on sofas!

What is loafing?

When a cat is "loafing," they have all four paws tucked underneath their body, resembling a loaf of bread. Cats often loaf when they are resting, and it is also a method for them to keep warm.

Why do cats love to loaf?

  • Comfort: The loaf position allows cats to stay warm and cozy.
  • Security: When a cat feels like they are in a safe place, they will retract their vicious claws and tuck them under their body. So, not only are they safe, but so are you!
  • Disguise: Sometimes cats will act as if they are in a relaxed loaf, drawing you in with their cuteness. However, this is used as an attack tactic, as they won't hesitate to pounce and devour your hand.

Where do cats love to loaf?

A very popular positions for cats to loaf is on top of sofas! Many cats prefer the specifically the top so they can pounce on you after they have decided their relaxing loaf time is over.
